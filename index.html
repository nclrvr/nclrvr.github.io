Nicaela Rivera

I'm a web and graphic designer.
I design and create websites, icons, prints, and logos.


I was born and raised in Manila, Philippines, and currently based in the Bay Area. In March 2014, I graduated with a BS in Management Information Systems with a specialization in Enterprise Systems. Since then I have involved myself in projects that married my love for visual design and fascination with technology.

My experience is junior-level but I know have a lot to offer. Minimalist designs and flat illustrations are my specialties.

On free times I like to read fantasy literature or binge-watch shows.

 Spell Cheese iOS App

 


App Mascot:


Color and Patterns:


Screenshots:

Lorenzo Rey Vergara
App Developer

Project Type:

Mobile, iOS App, Graphic Design, Asset Design


Software/s:

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator


Description:

Spell Cheese aims to make the process of learning words easier and more engaging for its young users. It allows the parents to take photos that their children are most familiar with; images that they see in real-life, scenarios that they experience first-hand, people that they meet and interact with.

As the graphic designer, my main goal was to make the visuals appealing to a child's eye. In order to achieve this, the direction we followed involved the creation of the following:

  1. Bright color scheme and backgrounds
  2. App mascot
  3. 5 Sets of the Alphabet with unique colorful patterns
