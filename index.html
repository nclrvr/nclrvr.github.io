H E L L O !


 

My name is Nica. I've been dabbling in web and graphic design for a while now. I create websites, icons, logos, illustrations, and have just on hand-lettering projects. My experience is pretty much junior-level but I think I have quite a lot to offer. Minimalist designs and flat illustrations are favorites of mine.


On off days and I'm not sitting in front of a computer screen I'm either: sitting and reading some book or comic, sketching while plopped on a chair, or, really, just relaxing. Sometimes I walk around too, but only if I have to.
